## 🏛️ 1. CDSCO Regulatory & Manufacturing Framework (India)
Before running commercial batches, we must ensure legal compliance under the **Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940** governed by the **Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO)**:

1. **Loan License (Form 32-A):** 
   * Since we outsource manufacturing to an OEM partner, their factory already holds a CDSCO Cosmetic Manufacturing License (Form 32).
   * We apply for a **Loan License (Form 32-A)**. This authorizes their factory to compound, fill, and pack our specific formulas under our brand labels.
   * **CDSCO Checklist:** Complete INCI ingredient list, COA (Certificate of Analysis) for all raw materials, and layout of the manufacturing facility.
2. **Labelling Compliance (Rule 148):**
   * Labels must clearly state: Brand name, product name, full INCI ingredient list, manufacturing license number (Mfg Lic No), batch number, manufacturing date (Mfg Date), expiry date (Exp Date), retail price (MRP incl. of all taxes), and the name and address of the loan licensee (us).
3. **Trademarking:**
   * File Class 3 trademarks for "SYNAPSE" and "BIOMINIMALIST" to protect brand names.

## 🔬 5. Phase-Gate Clinical Stability & Shelf Life Protocol
Before a single bottle from our loan-licensed factory can be legally sold to the public, the batch must pass three mandatory phase-gate clinical tests at an independent NABL-accredited laboratory:

```
[Batch Manufacture] ──► [90-Day Accelerated Chamber] ──► [Challenge Microbial Test] ──► [Human Patch (HRIPT)] ──► [CDSCO Release]
```

### Phase-Gate 1: Accelerated Thermal Stability (90-Day Chamber Run)
*   **The Test:** Samples from our initial run are placed in environmental chambers running at **4°C, 25°C (Ambient), and 45°C** at 60% relative humidity.
*   **What it proves:** 90 days at 45°C simulates **24 months of real-world shelf life**. The chemist checks the product weekly for phase separation, pH drifts, and color changes.

### Phase-Gate 2: Challenge Testing (PET - Preservative Efficacy Testing)
*   **The Test:** The laboratory deliberately inoculates our product bulk with active bacteria and fungi.
*   **What it proves:** Our preservative system must show a **99.9% reduction in microbes by Day 7** and maintain zero growth through Day 28.

### Phase-Gate 3: Human Repeat Insult Patch Test (HRIPT)
*   **The Test:** The formulation is patch-tested on **50 human volunteers** with sensitive skin under the direct supervision of a board-certified dermatologist.
*   **What it proves:** Zero allergic skin reactions or micro-peeling, allowing us to legally print **"Dermatologically Tested" and "Hypoallergenic"** on our packaging.
